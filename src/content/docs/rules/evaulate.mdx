### Evaluation Criteria

Each teams will be evaulated with following cr

1. **`Project Idea`** - 20 Marks
2. **`Thought, Creativity & Understanding`** - 10 Marks
3. **`Teamwork`** - 20 Marks
4. **`Presentation`** - 20 Marks
5. **`Q&A`** - 10 Marks
6. **`Organization & Completion`** - 10 Marks
7. **`Team Orientation`** - 10 Marks

_`Total Marks`_ - 100 Marks
